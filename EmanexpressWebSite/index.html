Our Services

One of our greatest advantages as a company is that we have structured our organization to operate with the strength, capacity, and power of a large corporation, yet still maintain the understanding and control of a San Bernardino, “Californian hometown carrier”.

Having the duality of knowing the local markets coupled with the expertise of local markets allows us to give you customized and personalized service at our maximum capability. That’s what makes us your “Hometown Californian Carrier”.

About EME

E Man Express stands for safe, secure, and reliable transportation services delivered by a network of independent and leased owner-operators, providing professionalism and securing our customers’ freight to create a reliable experience at all times. We empower these entrepreneurs to offer customized shipping solutions.

Our Vision

Our objective is to operate truckload businesses that are industry-leading in both margin and growth while providing cost-effective solutions for our customers.

To achieve these goals, we operate primarily in high-density, predictable freight lanes in select geographic regions within the state of California, and attempt to develop and expand our customer base around each of our services for each customer. This operating strategy allows us to take advantage of a large amount of freight transported in regional markets. Our centralized service in California enables us to better serve our customers and work more closely with our driving associates.

We operate a modern fleet to appeal to driving associates and customers, meticulously maintain our equipment to decrease downtime, and enhance our operating efficiencies.
